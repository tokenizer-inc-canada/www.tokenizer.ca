[Outside Intelligence Inc.](www.outsideiq.com) was our direct client from first days of inception till company acquisition by [Exiger (DDIQ)](https://www.exiger.com/DDIQ/).

We provided following services:
- Hadoop installations on Amazon EC2 instances, performance tuning 
- HBase installations on Amazon EC2 instances, performance tuning, schema design
- Cassandra installations on Amazon EC2 instances, schema design
- Data Acquisition: Apache Nutch, customized "Robot" implementation, proprietory database connectors
- Data Indexing and Search: Lucene/Solr
- Data Postprocessing, Machine Learning, Tagging, Categorization, and Indexing
